OUR STORY

About Esal Ventures

Founded on January 13, 2025, by visionary tech entrepreneurs Ednah Chessa and Salim Wamukoya, Esal Ventures is a Nairobi-based technology consultancy specializing in AI-driven business transformation.

Our mission is to empower enterprises by leveraging artificial intelligence to optimize operations, reduce costs, and gain a competitive edge in the digital era.

Our Vision

To be a leading catalyst in the integration of artificial intelligence within businesses, driving innovation and sustainable growth across industries.

Our Mission

To deliver cutting-edge AI solutions that transform business operations, enhance decision-making, and foster measurable value for our clients.
