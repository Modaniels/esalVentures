Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted business consulting partner across Africa.

Who We Are

Pioneering Business Excellence in Africa

Founded in 2008, EsalVentures began as a small team of passionate consultants with a vision to transform African businesses through strategic thinking and operational excellence.

Today, we've grown into a leading consulting firm with a presence in 8 African countries, having helped over 150 organizations achieve measurable growth and efficiency improvements.

Our approach combines global best practices with deep local market understanding, creating solutions that are both innovative and practical for the African business landscape.

Our Journey

Milestones & Achievements

2008 - Founding

EsalVentures was founded in Nairobi with a team of 5 consultants focused on procurement optimization for SMEs.

2012 - First Major Contract

Won our first government contract to transform procurement processes for a national ministry, establishing our public sector expertise.

2015 - Regional Expansion

Expanded operations to Tanzania and Uganda, marking the beginning of our pan-African presence.

2018 - Digital Transformation

Launched our digital transformation practice to help clients adapt to the Fourth Industrial Revolution.

2023 - Today

Now operating in 8 African countries with a team of 50+ consultants serving clients across multiple industries.
